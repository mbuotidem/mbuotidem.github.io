Where to get free svgs for presentations

  • 7th Oct 2024
  • •1 min read
  • •Tags: 
  • Presentations, 
  • SVG

You can get excellent SVG's at svgrepo

Sometimes, you gotta make a slide deck. And you need icons or vectors to make your presentation come alive. This article was going to be about using prompting and chatgpt to get the svg you need and validating and downloading it using svgviewer.dev.

You can of course still absolutely do that. But before you do, check svgrepo.com first. Everything you need might be there already, under an MIT License, and with no one showing you premium content while you just want to find free assets.

Thank goodness for open source!
