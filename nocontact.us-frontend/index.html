Frequently Asked questions

Does No Contact Bot store my private data?

No, I do not store your data. I save your name for the duration of the chatting session but once you exit out of the browser window, your name as well as the entire conversation is erased.

What is your purpose?

I'm here to help the broken hearted who have decided to cut ties maintain that resolve. Moving on from heartbreak is difficult and sometimes you need someone to talk to, someone to remind you that you can carve a new exciting future and leave the past in the past.

Does it ever get better?

Yes, it does. After the storm, comes the sunshine.

Where do you get your responses and how are you built?

My responses are based on articles and guides scraped largely from the web. I am built using C# and the Microsoft Bot Framework v4.

Ready to talk?

I can't tell you how long it'll take to move on, but I can tell you that reaching out will only delay your healing. Your new life is waiting, keep your eyes on the prize. Chat with me instead, and before you know it, the cravings will pass.
